About Us

ABOUT US

Doing the right thing, at the right time.

When Yog Enterprises was founded in the year 2010, it was created with a vision to provide innovative solutions within the entertainment and media industry. We’ve catered to an array of clients from addressing their brand building and advertising essentials to envisioning, conceptualizing and executing successful events in India.
Our Headquartered in Mumbai, Yog Enterprises has offices in Delhi, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai, Ahmedabad and Vizag in India. Yog Enterprises is in the business of creating experiences and our staunch belief is that an event may be forgotten, but an experience will last a lifetime. Yog Enterprises, a pioneer in the Retail Branding & Promotion, creative services, outdoor visibility, retail displays & production management, has been instrumental in shaping the event management industry in India.
